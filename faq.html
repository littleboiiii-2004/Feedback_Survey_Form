FAQs

Can I edit or update my survey responses after submission?

Unfortunately, once you submit your responses, they cannot be edited.
Please double-check your answers before submitting to ensure accuracy.

How often are new surveys available?

We strive to provide a variety of surveys regularly.
The frequency may vary, but we aim to offer new opportunities for feedback on a consistent basis.

Is my feedback anonymous?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. While we may ask for some personal information to understand our user base better,
your feedback will be kept anonymous unless you choose to provide identifiable information voluntarily.

Can I contact someone if I have issues with the survey or website?

Absolutely! Visit the "Contact Us" page to reach out.
You can fill out the contact form, and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.
